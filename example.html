draggable

Here is an example draggable div that will simply move anywhere you want it to in the window.

You can also restrict how a draggable can be dragged to vertically, horizontally or both.

This is a contained draggable

You can also add a ghosting effect to your draggable

We also have the concept of pens which are elements that other draggables can be dropped into and taken out of.

The blue one can be dragged anywhere but will snap to its pens. The purple one is restricted to being within one of its declared pens. Try it and see.
